User Guides

Getting Started
  1. Create an account on the login page.
  2. Sign in to access your dashboard.
  3. Use the converter, alerts, history, and news workspace.
Using the Converter
  1. Search or select the source currency.
  2. Choose the target currency.
  3. Enter an amount and convert.
  4. Review the result and saved history entry.
Setting Alerts
  1. Go to Alerts.
  2. Choose the pair and target rate.
  3. Save the alert and monitor it from the app.
Reading Market Context
  1. Open News.
  2. Review the live market tiles.
  3. Filter by topic for specific news coverage.
